Rock Paper Scissors with the Grim Reaper

You have one chance to save your life

You're walking to your favorite lunch place, when you notice the kid next to you darting the street after his basketball that rolled down the street. There's a truck coming down the street.

Based on pure instinct, you chase after the kid and push him onto the sidewalk.

Everything suddenly stops. You sense a presences next to you.

You turn around and see the Grim Reaper next to you.

"Am I dead?" you ask

"No," he says, "you are in the space between life and death right now, but you will be soon."

"Since you lived your life to your fullest and your life is in jeapordy because you did a good deed, I can give you one chance to save your life."

"Play a game of rock, paper, scissors with me."

"If you win, it'll be like nothing happened. You'll go back to living your life until the next time we meet."

"If you lose, I'll have to take you back to the afterlife as planned."
